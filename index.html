KAZILI KUYUM

Here is my final project for MUSIC-270! I wanted to make a little page to talk a little bit about what it was like to work on this project.

This song is one of my favorites, and so I really wanted to challenge myself with it. The chords I found online were disappointing, so I thought I'd figure it out by ear. I wasn't about to be discouraged. Once I got the idea, I transposed it so that it would be within my singing range.

But transposition has never been my strong suit, so naturally, I messed that up many many times, and just couldn't figure out why the song wasn't sounding right. At a certain point, I think I gave up on transposing it right; as long as it didn't sound too off, I stopped caring whether it was true to the original or not. I really didn't have the stamina to record the vocals all over again, as I got myself sick in the process.

I've been working on this for about three weeks now, and I began my journey pretty hopeful about covering the whole song. Turns out I had no idea how tough it would be to record the vocals! It took me many many recordings, re-recordings, and I started over many many times. It was honestly brutal.

As I was trying to figure out my way through all these, I realized it would be better for my sake to do only about half of the song, as figuring out the rest by ear would be very difficult.

And so here it is! I hope you enjoy it!
